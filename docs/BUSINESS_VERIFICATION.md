# WhatsApp / Meta Business Verification — Checklist & Steps

**Why:** unlocks the business **display name** ("Total Facilities Management
Limited") showing to customers instead of the raw number, raises the messaging
limit above the ~250/day dev cap, and is required for production. It is a Meta
account action — the app needs no change; the display name is already set.

## Prepare first (≈ 99% of rejections are detail mismatches)

- [ ] **Legal business name, address, phone** — written down **exactly** as they
      appear on the official document (spacing, punctuation, "Limited" vs "Ltd").
- [ ] **Verification document** — for Nigeria the **CAC Certificate of
      Incorporation** is cleanest. Alternatives: a recent (< 90 days) **business
      bank statement** or **utility bill in the business's name**. Must be legible,
      unexpired, and in the business name (not personal).
- [ ] **Business website / domain** that references the business (helps a lot).
- [ ] **Business email** — ideally on that domain.
- [ ] **A phone number Meta can call or SMS** for a code.
- [ ] Some **web presence** (site, listing, or social) so the business is findable.

## Steps

1. **business.facebook.com** → **Business Settings** (gear icon) → **Security
   Center** (or **Business Info**).
2. Find **Business Verification** → **Start verification**.
3. **Confirm business details** — name, address, phone. **Match the document
   letter-for-letter.**
4. **Choose a method** — appear in an official database, or **upload a document**.
   Upload the CAC certificate (or the alternative).
5. **Verify a phone number or email** with the code Meta sends.
6. **Submit.** Review is usually minutes to a few days (occasionally up to ~2 weeks).
7. **Track status** in Security Center; watch email for follow-ups.

## After it's approved

- The WhatsApp **display-name review** completes → the org name replaces the number
  for customers who don't have it saved.
- If you need more than ~250 conversations/day, request a higher messaging tier in
  WhatsApp Manager.

## Common rejection reasons (avoid these)

- Typed name/address **doesn't match** the document.
- Document **unreadable, expired, or in a personal name**.
- **No web presence** for the business.
- Personal rather than business details.

## Dual-brand note

Verification is per **Meta Business Portfolio**. The current portfolio is **TFML**,
so this verifies the TFML identity. **OEA** ("Ora Egbunike & Associates") needs its
**own** verification only if it is a separate legal entity with its own number /
WhatsApp Business Account — which pairs with the Phase-1 per-brand channel work.
